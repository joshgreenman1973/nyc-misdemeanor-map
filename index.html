The Precinct Ledger

Every misdemeanor and violation the New York Police Department recorded from 2015 through the first quarter of 2026 — more than 3.7 million complaints and 1.6 million arrests — down to the precinct. For petit larceny or assault, complaints and arrests roughly track each other. For drug possession, trespassing and fare evasion, they don't: those counts reflect enforcement decisions as much as what actually happened.
